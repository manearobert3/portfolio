# Privacy Policy

_Last updated: March 23, 2025_

Thank you for visiting my portfolio website. Your privacy is important to me. This privacy policy explains how your personal data is handled when you submit a message through the contact form on this site.

---

## 1. Information I Collect

When you use the contact form, you may be asked to provide:

- Your **name**
- Your **email address**
- Your **message**

This information is necessary for me to receive and respond to your inquiry.

---

## 2. How Your Information is Handled

- I use [**Resend**](https://resend.com) to securely send contact form submissions directly to my email inbox.
- **I do not store**, log, or track your personal data (such as name, email, or message content) on any server or database.
- This website **does not have a backend** — it is a static portfolio site hosted with no data storage mechanisms.

---

## 3. Data Sharing

- Your data is **not shared**, sold, or transferred to any third parties.
- The email service provider (**Resend**) only processes the form submission to forward it to my inbox. You can read their [Privacy Policy](https://resend.com/legal/privacy-policy) for more details.

---

## 4. Cookies and Tracking

- This site does **not use cookies**, analytics tools, or any third-party trackers.

---

## 5. Your Rights

Since no data is stored, there's no need for you to request access, deletion, or correction of your information — nothing is retained after your message is sent.

---

## 6. Contact

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or your data, feel free to reach out to me directly via the contact form.

---

> By using the contact form, you acknowledge and agree to the terms outlined in this Privacy Policy.
